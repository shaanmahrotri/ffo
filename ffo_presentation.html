Executive Summary

Mission & Vision

Mission: Serve modern wealth with a modular family office model that delivers institutional-grade expertise at a fraction of traditional costs.
Vision: Redefine accessibility and efficiency in wealth management for the next generation of wealth creators and inheritors.
Why Now? Surge in new wealth creation, rise of alternatives, and increasing demand for transparency and digital solutions.

Value Proposition & FFO Model

The FFO Concept

Fractional Family Office

A modern wealth management solution delivering family office capabilities without the overhead of a full-time dedicated team.

Traditional SFO Alternative: Comprehensive services at 30-40% of traditional SFO costs
For Modern Wealth: Tailored for £75M-£200M families seeking institutional-grade solutions
Client Control: Clients maintain decision authority while leveraging FFO expertise

Key Differentiators

True Independence: No product sales, commission-based incentives, or hidden fees
Tech-Enabled: Proprietary client dashboard for portfolio oversight and performance monitoring
Modular Structure: Services scaled up/down as family needs evolve over time

Core Service Modules

Investment Oversight

Portfolio construction, manager selection, asset allocation, performance reporting, and co-investment opportunities

Wealth Structuring

Tax-efficient entity structures, estate planning coordination, philanthropy advisory, and succession planning

Reporting Consolidation

Aggregated performance metrics, tax documentation, risk analysis, and custom reporting across all assets

Family Governance

Next-gen education, family meeting facilitation, mission/value alignment, and conflict resolution

Client Adoption Pattern

Typical clients begin with 2-3 core services and add additional modules within 18 months

Operating Model & Partnership Strategy

Outsourced Specialist Network

Curated Expert Network: Rigorously vetted specialists delivering services under unified FFO brand.
Quality Control: Partners undergo quarterly performance reviews and maintain exclusive client relationships.
Client Experience: Single point of contact managing all partner relationships for seamless service delivery.

CIO-Led Investment Committee

Expert Oversight: Senior CIO chairs committee of seasoned investment professionals with specialized expertise.
Structured Process: Bi-weekly meetings, formal voting protocols, and detailed investment memoranda.
Risk Management: Comprehensive scoring framework for all investments with quarterly performance reviews.

FFO Operating Principles

Independent & Unconflicted

No proprietary products, commission-free advice, transparent fee structure.

Client-Centric Design

Services customized to family needs rather than standardized offerings.

Institutional-Grade Security

Enterprise-level data protection and privacy standards across all operations.
